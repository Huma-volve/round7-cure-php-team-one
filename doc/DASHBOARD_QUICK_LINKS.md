# Dashboard Quick Links Guide

## Admin Dashboard Links

### Main Dashboard
- **URL**: `/admin/dashboard`
- **Description**: View overall statistics including users, bookings, payments, and disputes. See charts and recent bookings.

---

## Admin Management Section

### Users Management
- **URL**: `/admin/users`
- **Description**: List, create, edit, and delete users. Manage user roles and permissions. View user details and payment methods.

### Doctors Management
- **URL**: `/admin/doctors`
- **Description**: Manage all doctors. Create, edit, delete doctors. View doctor profile, bookings, patients, earnings, and toggle status.

---

## Admin Operations Section

### Patients Management
- **URL**: `/admin/patients`
- **Description**: Manage all patients. Create, edit, delete patients. View patient booking history and payment methods.

### Bookings Management
- **URL**: `/admin/bookings`
- **Description**: View all bookings. Filter by status and date range. Edit booking details, update status, and manage payment information.

### Payments Monitoring
- **URL**: `/admin/payments`
- **Description**: Monitor all payments. View payment details, transaction IDs, gateway information, and process refunds.

### Disputes Management
- **URL**: `/admin/disputes`
- **Description**: Manage payment and booking disputes. Resolve disputes, add notes, and track dispute history.

### Support Tickets
- **URL**: `/admin/tickets`
- **Description**: Handle customer support tickets. View ticket details, reply to tickets, and update ticket status.

### FAQs Management
- **URL**: `/admin/faqs`
- **Description**: Create, edit, delete frequently asked questions. Manage both English and Arabic translations.

---

## Admin Settings Section

### Settings
- **URL**: `/admin/settings`
- **Description**: Configure system settings including app name, logo, contact information, social media links, and payment gateway credentials.

---

## Admin Account Menu (Top Right Dropdown)

### Notifications
- **URL**: `/admin/notifications`
- **Description**: View all notifications. Mark notifications as read or mark all as read.

### Profile
- **URL**: `/admin/account/profile`
- **Description**: View and update your profile information including name, email, and profile photo.

### Settings
- **URL**: `/admin/account/settings`
- **Description**: Change your password, update account settings, and change interface language (English/Arabic).

### Activity Log
- **URL**: `/admin/account/activity-log`
- **Description**: View history of your account activities including logins, profile updates, and password changes.

### Logout
- **Description**: Sign out from the admin panel.

---

## Doctor Dashboard Links

### Main Dashboard
- **URL**: `/admin/doctor/dashboard`
- **Description**: View your statistics including total bookings, upcoming bookings, total patients, and earnings. See recent bookings list.

---

## Doctor Menu Section

### Bookings
- **URL**: `/admin/doctor/bookings`
- **Description**: View all your bookings. Filter by status. Confirm pending bookings, cancel bookings, and reschedule appointments.

### Patients
- **URL**: `/admin/doctor/patients`
- **Description**: View list of patients who booked appointments with you. See patient names, contact information, and booking history.

### Payments
- **URL**: `/admin/doctor/payments`
- **Description**: View all payments received for your bookings. See payment details, transaction IDs, and payment methods used.

### Schedule
- **URL**: `/admin/doctor/schedule`
- **Description**: Set and manage your working hours. Define start and end times for each day of the week to control booking availability.

---

## Navigation Tips

### For Admins:
1. **Start with Dashboard** to see system overview
2. **Check Notifications** to respond to alerts
3. **Manage Users** to control access
4. **Monitor Payments** to track revenue
5. **Resolve Disputes** promptly to maintain platform trust
6. **Update Settings** to configure system

### For Doctors:
1. **Check Dashboard** for daily statistics
2. **Review Bookings** to confirm appointments
3. **Update Schedule** to control availability
4. **Track Payments** to monitor earnings
5. **View Patients** to understand client base
6. **Check Notifications** for urgent messages
